The Story
We are elect, a company based out of West Lafayette, Indiana.
We are Ainesh, Elijah, and Anurag.

Elijah suffers from extreme allergies which drastically limited the places he could eat. We ended up spending a lot of time looking for places where we could all eat together, and many times Elijah would end up having to eat at home anyway. This gave us the idea to create elect. What if there was one website you could visit, to easily find all the places that serve food you can actually eat? We immediately began working on this concept because we understand how difficult it can be to find food when you have allergies or any dietary needs.
